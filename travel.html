ii. What I make

Four kinds of stories, quietly told.

Format 01

Travel reels & vlogs

Cinematic, slow, with the kind of pacing that makes you want to be there.

  • 30–90 second vertical reels for Instagram & TikTok
  • 2–5 minute long-form vlogs for YouTube
  • Native voiceover, captioning, color grade in CapCut
Format 02

UGC for brands

Product-in-context content that feels like a friend's recommendation, not a feed ad.

  • Outdoor gear, hospitality, travel accessories

Diary writing

Words for newsletters, captions, blog posts. Tender, careful, never overwritten.

Hosted features

In-feed dedicated content with a real point of view.
