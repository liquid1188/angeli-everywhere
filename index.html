i. A short introduction

I make calm out of inboxes
and cinema out of the everyday.

— self-portrait, between projects

My friends call me Angie. My CV says Angeli. Either is fine — I answer to both.

I trained as a physical therapist before I ever wrote a subject line that made someone smile. The same instinct, really: pay close attention, move with care, follow the work where it wants to go. I just trade clinics for inboxes now.

For five years I have lived inside the quiet rooms of business — administration, operations, client support, executive assistance. I'm warm, but I'm not soft. I send the awkward email. I keep the calendar honest. I tell you when something isn't working.
