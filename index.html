01 — About

A working diary

Angeli — but also Angel, also Angie, depending on who's calling and from which time zone. A Filipino freelancer with a Bachelor's in Physical Therapy and a slightly winding career through administration, operations, and executive assistance. Each role taught her the same lesson in a different language: pay attention, then move quietly.

Now she works at the intersection of the two things she's always done well — taking care of the unseen scaffolding that makes someone else's day work, and watching the world long enough to film it back honestly. Travel reels with no overt sell. Operations with no friction. Client emails written like letters.

She is selective. She'd rather decline than do the wrong work. The brand she's building lives at @angeli.everywhere across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube — and the goal, eventually, is a small constellation of digital products and brand collaborations that feel earned.

Clarity over cleverness. Authentic over salesy. Polished, but never plastic.
