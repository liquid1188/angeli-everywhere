About Angeli

I trained as a physical therapist before I learned to write a subject line that made someone smile. The same instinct, really — pay close attention, move with care, follow the body of the work where it wants to go. I just trade clinics for inboxes now, and shoulders for shoulders of meaning.

For years I have worked the quiet rooms of business: administration, operations, client support, executive assistance. I learned that good work is mostly noticing. The unfinished thread. The calendar collision. The tone a client uses when they are tired and need someone gentler. I am the person who notices.

Lately I am building something of my own. A creator brand stitched from travel light and slow stories — @angeli.everywhere. I film with what I have. I write the way I think: diary-like, a little tender, careful with words. I am picky about what I make and who I make it with, because clarity and authentic storytelling matter more to me than reach.

If you are here, you are probably looking for someone steady. Someone who can run the calendar and name the brand. I am both of those things, often in the same afternoon.
