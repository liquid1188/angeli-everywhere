v. How we work

The process, from inquiry to invoice.

Step 01 · 2–3 days

Inquiry & reply

— I read everything personally

You send the form. I reply within 2–3 business days, either with a yes-let's-talk, or a no-but-here's-someone-better. No silent inbox.

Step 02 · 30 minutes

Discovery call

— Free, no pressure

We hop on Zoom. You tell me what's broken or building. I ask the kind of questions that surface the real problem. Either of us can say "not a fit" without it being awkward.

Step 03 · 48 hrs

Proposal & scope

— Honest about hours and rate

You get a one-page scope: what I'll do, what I won't, what it costs, when it ships. No corporate boilerplate. Plain English.

Step 04 · ongoing

Work & rhythm

— Slow is smooth. Smooth is fast.

Weekly Loom check-ins or async updates, your preference. Invoiced on the 1st and 15th. We can pause, scale, or close anytime.

vi. Frequently asked

Things people ask first.

Are you a Filipino VA? How is this different from OnlineJobs.ph?

Yes — Manila-based, B.S. Physical Therapy, five years professional admin and ops experience. The difference is taste. I write English at a near-native level, I push back when an idea isn't working, and I treat your brand voice like it's mine. I cost more than the platform average because I'm worth more than the platform average.

What time zones do you cover?

I keep EU hours by default and shift earlier or later for North American clients. I currently overlap daily with Berlin, London, New York, and the Philippines. I'll never pretend I'm awake when I'm not.

Will you sign an NDA?

Yes — happy to. I work with founders on early-stage products and I take confidentiality seriously. Send me yours, or I can send a simple mutual NDA.

Do you take one-off projects?

Yes for content (UGC, naming, brand sprints). Mostly no for ops and EA — those work better as retainers because the value compounds. I'll tell you straight if a one-off doesn't make sense.

What's your invoicing setup?

Wise, PayPal, or direct bank transfer for retainers. Half upfront for project work. I send clean line-item invoices on the 1st and 15th of each month for ongoing engagements.

Are you taking on UGC brand deals?

Yes, selectively. I prefer travel brands, outdoor gear, hospitality, and slow-living products. I'm not the right fit for fast fashion, alcohol, or anything that needs comedic skit energy. Pitch me at the inquiry form.

Tell me what you're building. I'll reply within 2–3 business days.
