Process, simplified.

No mystery, no surprises. Here's exactly how a project moves from inquiry to handoff.

01

Inquiry

You send a project brief. I read it personally and reply within 2–3 business days if there's a fit.

A 30-minute call (free) to confirm scope, voice, expectations, and whether we genuinely want to work together.

03

A short, plain-English proposal with deliverables, timeline, and price. Half-deposit to start.

Work, then handoff
